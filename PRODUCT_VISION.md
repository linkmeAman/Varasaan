# 🕊️ Digital Legacy Manager — Product Vision & Goals

> **"We don't break into accounts. We help families close, recover, and preserve digital life — legally, fast, and with proof."**

---

## 🎯 The Problem We Solve

When someone dies, their family faces two simultaneous crises:

1. **Emotional** — grief, shock, funeral arrangements
2. **Administrative** — a bureaucratic maze of locked devices, platform gatekeepers, unknown subscriptions, and inaccessible accounts

The current reality for families in India:

- No centralised way to discover what digital accounts exist
- Platforms (Google, Apple, Meta) require legal documentation most families don't know how to prepare
- Demat accounts, PF, and insurance payouts stall because email/OTP access is locked
- Crypto and wallets may be permanently lost if seed phrases aren't documented
- Recurring payments continue billing dead people's cards for months

**Nobody is building this for India.** US players haven't localised for DPDP, demat accounts, Hindu Succession Act, or the reality that most Indian families don't have lawyers on retainer.

---

## 🌟 Product Vision

**Build the operating system for digital bereavement in India.**

A case management platform — not a vault, not a password manager — that guides families through every step of closing, recovering, and preserving a deceased person's digital life.

Two modes:

| Mode | Who Uses It | When |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Planning Mode** | Individual user | While alive |
| **After-Loss Mode** | Family / executor | After death is confirmed |

## 🔐 Core Principles (Non-Negotiable)

### 1. We Never Touch Credentials
We help families submit **authorised requests** to platforms. We never log into accounts on anyone's behalf. This protects users legally and keeps us out of CFAA/IT Act territory.

### 2. Privacy Is Infrastructure, Not a Feature
We handle death certificates, IDs, legal documents. DPDP compliance is not a checkbox — it's the foundation.

```
- Encryption at rest for all documents
- Client-side encryption for sensitive vaults
- Short retention policies (not indefinitely storing data)
- Breach response plan from Day 1
```

### 3. Non-Custodial for Crypto
We never control funds or keys. We help users document and split secrets using Shamir-style approaches. If we can't do it without custody, we don't do it.

### 4. Legal Safety Over Feature Velocity
We won't ship features that expose users or ourselves to legal risk, even if they seem useful. "Auto-delete all accounts" is a trap. "Here is exactly how to request deletion" is the product.
